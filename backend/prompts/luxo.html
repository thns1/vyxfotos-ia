Tema 02 — Luxo

Luxo

10 Prompts Profissionais · 2 Subtemas · 5 Variações Cada

01
Luxo Clássico
Variação 01 — Black Bespoke
Ultra-high quality luxury prestige portrait photograph. The subject wears a black bespoke suit of exceptional quality with fine lapels, a silk tie in deep charcoal, and accessories suggesting generational wealth — a distinguished luxury timepiece, understated gold or platinum cufflinks. Background is a private library with floor-to-ceiling leather-bound books, dark mahogany shelving, and ambient candlelight creating a profound atmosphere of old-world luxury. Moody, dramatic lighting with deep shadows and precise highlights. Photorealistic, editorial luxury fashion quality, 8K.
Variação 02 — Old Money
Exclusive prestige portrait in the tradition of old-money elegance. The subject wears a perfectly tailored charcoal or black suit of the finest fabric, a white dress shirt with discreet monogram, and a silk tie in a conservative luxury pattern. Background is a private club or heritage estate interior — oil paintings, fine antiques, warm wood paneling, softly blurred. Classic portrait lighting reminiscent of old masters. Photorealistic, museum-quality aesthetic.
Variação 03 — Heritage Estate
Heritage luxury portrait photograph. The subject wears a timeless black suit with silk lapel detail — a tuxedo or smoking jacket — paired with a crisp white shirt and understated luxury accessories. Background is a grand private estate interior: sweeping marble staircase, crystal chandeliers, or a formal drawing room, softly blurred. Cinematic lighting with rich shadows and highlights emphasizing the subject's aristocratic presence. Photorealistic, 8K, luxury fashion editorial quality.
Variação 04 — Connoisseur
Collector and connoisseur portrait. The subject wears a distinguished dark suit — perhaps with a subtle velvet or silk texture detail — projecting the image of a refined collector and patron of the arts. Background is a private gallery or distinguished interior with carefully curated objects, softly blurred. Controlled, gallery-style lighting creating a sense of quiet, confident luxury. Photorealistic, ultra-refined aesthetic.
Variação 05 — Grand Gala
Grand gala luxury portrait. The subject wears formal black tie attire — a perfectly cut tuxedo with satin lapels, crisp white dress shirt, black bow tie, and all appropriate luxury accessories. Background is a grand gala ballroom with golden chandeliers, opulent drapery, and warm festive light, softly blurred. Elegant portrait lighting with warm tones and rich depth. Photorealistic, 8K, suitable for an exclusive society event portrait.
02
Premium — Luz Quente
Variação 01 — Golden Hour
Ultra-high quality warm golden luxury portrait photograph. The subject is dressed in elegant attire in warm champagne or gold tones — a luxurious fabric suit, dress, or evening wear with fine accessories in gold or warm metals. Background bathes in rich golden hour light: a terrace overlooking an exclusive luxury landscape, or golden hour flooding through floor-to-ceiling windows of a penthouse. Rich, warm color palette of honey, amber, and champagne. Soft bokeh background, cinematic depth of field. Photorealistic, beauty editorial quality, 8K.
Variação 02 — Penthouse Sunset
Premium golden hour luxury portrait. The subject wears sophisticated warm-toned attire — cream, ivory, or champagne — with fine jewelry and luxury accessories reflecting warm light. Background is a penthouse terrace or rooftop pool at dusk, the city glowing in golden twilight behind them, softly blurred. The entire scene radiates warmth and exclusivity. Soft natural light with warm amber fill. Photorealistic, luxury lifestyle magazine quality.
Variação 03 — Champagne Night
Warm light prestige portrait. The subject wears rich, warm-toned luxury attire — perhaps a caramel, bronze, or warm taupe suit or dress — with gold jewelry and accessories. Background is an exclusive champagne lounge, gold-accented interior, or a private party in a grand venue with warm festive lighting, softly blurred. The atmosphere is celebratory, opulent, and warm. Soft candle-like lighting with golden tones. Photorealistic, 8K.
Variação 04 — Amber Elegance
Amber elegance luxury portrait. The subject wears sophisticated evening attire in warm tones — dark navy or black with gold accessories, or rich jewel-toned dress with gold jewelry. Background is a private dinner or exclusive tasting event with warm amber candle lighting, softly blurred tables and crystal glassware visible. The scene evokes the finest dining experience. Warm, intimate lighting with precise face illumination. Photorealistic, ultra-luxury aesthetic.
Variação 05 — Gilded Portrait
Gilded prestige portrait in the tradition of Renaissance portraiture. The subject wears opulent attire with warm metallic tones — a blazer in gold or bronze, or formal wear with luxury accessories in gold. Background suggests extreme wealth: golden decorative elements, fine art, or luxury interior in warm amber and gold tones, softly blurred. Rich, painterly lighting reminiscent of Rembrandt but with modern luxury sensibility. Photorealistic, 8K, gallery-quality aesthetic.
