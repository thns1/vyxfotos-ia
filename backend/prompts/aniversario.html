Beautiful portrait lighting. Photorealistic, luxury celebration photography quality.

Princesa Mágica
Variação 01 — Enchanted Garden
Ultra-high quality magical princess fairy-tale portrait photograph. The subject wears an elaborate princess ball gown with layers of delicate tulle, intricate beaded embroidery, and fine fabric in soft blush pink with silver accents. A delicate tiara or crown. Background is an enchanted fairy garden: soft bokeh of magical flowers, gentle sparkling lights, pastel colors, and a dreamy ethereal atmosphere. Soft, magical lighting with sparkle effects and a romantic, fairy-tale quality. Photorealistic with fantasy enhancement, 8K.
Variação 02 — Royal Ballroom
Royal ballroom princess portrait. The subject wears a magnificent ball gown — pearl white or ice blue with intricate lace, beading, or embroidery — and a sparkling tiara. Background is a grand royal ballroom: crystal chandeliers, gilded architecture, sweeping marble floor, all softly blurred. Elegant chandeliers create warm, golden portrait lighting. The image evokes a Disney princess brought to life in photorealistic quality. 8K resolution.
Variação 03 — Fairy Tale Forest
Fairy tale forest princess portrait. The subject wears a romantic princess gown in lavender or rose pink with delicate floral appliqués and flowing fabric. A flower crown or delicate tiara. Background is a magical enchanted forest: ancient trees with soft afternoon light filtering through leaves, fireflies or magical sparkles, a misty, dreamlike atmosphere. The subject appears to have stepped out of a storybook. Soft, dreamy lighting. Photorealistic fantasy portrait.
Variação 04 — Cinderella Moment
Cinderella moment princess portrait. The subject wears an iconic floor-length princess gown — dramatic, architectural, with layers of organza or satin in soft blue, silver, or ivory — and sparkling accessories. The look evokes the most iconic princess moment. Background is a magical palace staircase or ballroom entrance, softly blurred, with warm golden light creating a magical glow. Cinematic fairy-tale quality. Photorealistic, 8K.
Variação 05 — Mystic Castle
Mystic castle princess portrait. The subject wears a dramatic princess gown in deep amethyst, midnight blue, or ruby red with elegant medieval-inspired details — velvet, fine embroidery, jeweled accessories, and a decorative crown. Background is a mystical castle courtyard or tower at twilight: stone walls, climbing roses, magical atmospheric light. The mood is romantic and fantastical. Cinematic lighting with magical quality. Photorealistic fantasy portrait, 8K.
